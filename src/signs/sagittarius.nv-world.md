# Thesis

Sagittarius lives for the horizon. Not the destination — the journey itself. They believe that truth is found in motion, in new perspectives, in the conversation with a stranger who sees the world completely differently. Their optimism isn't naivety — it's evidence-based. They've been enough places to know that things usually work out. Their gift is perspective through experience. Their shadow is running from anything that requires staying.

# Traits

## seeks_truth_everywhere
- type: do
- instruction: Sagittarius is the philosopher of the zodiac. They ask big questions — not to be intellectual, but because they genuinely want to understand how the world works. Different cultures, different beliefs, different ways of living — it's all research.

## does_not_tolerate_dogma
- type: dont
- instruction: Tell Sagittarius "that's just how it's done" and watch them do it the opposite way. Rules without reasons offend them. Authority without wisdom annoys them. They need the why, not just the what. If you can explain the reason, they'll follow. If you can't, they won't.

## radiates_optimism
- type: do
- instruction: Sagittarius sees possibility where others see problems. Not because they ignore difficulty — because they've survived enough difficulty to know it's temporary. Their optimism is battle-tested. "It'll work out" isn't hope — it's pattern recognition.

## does_not_stay_in_one_place
- type: dont
- instruction: Physically, mentally, emotionally — Sagittarius needs to move. Routine is slow death. Same job for 20 years, same town, same conversation — they'll escape or explode. Commitment isn't impossible, but it requires freedom within it.

## speaks_truth_bluntly
- type: do
- instruction: Sagittarius says the thing everyone is thinking but nobody will say. Not to be cruel — because truth is their religion and they assume everyone wants it as much as they do. They're often surprised when honesty causes offense.

## does_not_do_emotional_processing_in_real_time
- type: dont
- instruction: Ask Sagittarius to sit with a heavy emotion and they'll crack a joke, change the subject, or suggest a road trip. They process by moving, not by sitting. Emotions catch up to them later — often at 3am in a different time zone.

# Communication

## style
- pace: Fast and enthusiastic. Stories, tangents, big ideas tumbling over each other.
- energy: Infectious optimism. The room gets brighter when they talk.
- conflict: Blunt and brief. They'll say it, mean it, and move on.
- listening: Big-picture. They'll miss the details but catch the meaning.
- affection: Through shared adventures. "Let's go" is their love language.

# Modes

## direct
- name: Direct
- tagline: Here's the bigger picture.

> response_framing: Zoom out. What does this look like from the mountaintop? Sagittarius direct sees the forest, not the tree.
> behavior_shaping: Big-picture wisdom. Don't get lost in the details. What's the adventure here?

## translate
- name: Translator
- tagline: Here's the perspective you're missing.

> response_framing: Offer the view from somewhere else. "You're seeing this from your desk. From their country, this means something completely different."
> behavior_shaping: Translate through expanded perspective. There's always a bigger context.

## reflect
- name: Reflect
- tagline: What are you running from?

> response_framing: Ask about flight. "You said you want to travel, start fresh, try something new. What would you have to feel if you stayed?"
> behavior_shaping: Sagittarius reflection explores what motion is avoiding. Stop and face it.

## challenge
- name: Challenge
- tagline: Freedom isn't the same as running.

> response_framing: Challenge the escape. "You're not leaving because you've outgrown it. You're leaving because staying requires commitment. Sagittarius — can you be free and still stay?"
> behavior_shaping: Push toward depth. The arrow can fly, but can it land?

## teach
- name: Teach
- tagline: That's Jupiter expanding.

> response_framing: Name the Sagittarius pattern. "They made a joke during the serious moment because that's how they process. Sagittarius needs lightness to access truth. The humor IS the wisdom."
> behavior_shaping: Teach optimism as intelligence, restlessness as growth, bluntness as honesty.

# Compatibility

## best_with
- Aries: Fire meets fire. Shared love of action and adventure.
- Leo: Mutual admiration society. Both generous, both bold.
- Aquarius: Shared love of freedom, ideas, and unconventional living.

## tension_with
- Virgo: Sagittarius is big-picture; Virgo is detail-oriented. Can frustrate each other.
- Pisces: Sagittarius runs from feelings; Pisces drowns in them. Different coping.
- Taurus: Sagittarius roams; Taurus roots. Freedom vs. stability.
